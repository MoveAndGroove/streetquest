Live Map Explorer

Real-time GPS tracking draws your route as you walk. Discover nearby art, parks, restaurants and hidden spots — all on a live map.

City Routes

71 curated walking routes across 14 cities — from Brooklyn Bridge to Beacon Hill. Each route has real waypoints, highlights, and insider notes.

Restaurant Roulette

Not sure where to go for date night? Spin the wheel to find a real nearby restaurant rated by locals! No more "I don't know, where do you want to go?"

Memory Map

Every place you check into becomes a photo pin on your map. Zoom out and see your entire city story — every neighborhood you've explored, one spot at a time.

Friends & Leaderboard

Invite friends and see who's exploring the most. Every city has its own leaderboard — climb the ranks as you check in, walk, and discover new spots together.

Walk a quarter mile every day to keep your streak alive. Miss a day and start over. Simple. Addictive. Effective.

Three new quests every day — from mystery spots with cryptic clues to timed distance challenges. Complete them to earn XP. Resets at midnight.

Mystery Spots

Hidden courtyards, forgotten staircases, secret gardens. Every city has places most people walk past every day. Street Quest helps you find them.

💡
Local Facts

Hyper-local trivia about your city automatically detected by GPS. Philadelphia? Liberty Bell facts. Boston? Revolution history. Wherever you are.
